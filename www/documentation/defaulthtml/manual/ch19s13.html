19.13. Fork

Fork is a pseudostate (see Section 19.8, “Pseudostate”) which splits a transition into two or more concurrent transitions.

Caution

The outgoing transitions should not have guards. However the V0.14 release of ArgoUML will not enforce this.

A fork is represented on the diagram as a solid (black by default) horizontal bar.

Tip

This bar can be made vertical by selecting the fork, and dragging with button 1 one of its corners.
