19.14. Join

Join is a pseudostate (see Section 19.8, “Pseudostate”) which joins two or more concurrent transitions into a single transition.

Caution

The incoming transitions should not have guards. However the V0.14 release of ArgoUML will not enforce this.

A join is represented on the diagram as a solid (black by default) horizontal bar.

Tip

This bar can be made vertical by selecting the join, and dragging with button 1 one of its corners.
