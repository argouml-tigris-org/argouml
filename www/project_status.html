ArgoUML project status

Roadmap

ArgoUML 0.9.6 is out, a new roadmap will be put online shortly!

Votes!

We are happy to serve our user community by giving them the chance to vote for items which they would like to get fixed in Issuezilla. Every registered user is eligable to enter a maximum of ten points per item, indicating therewith the desirability of the bug fix or feature request.

For a list of items which have been voted for look at the Votes!
